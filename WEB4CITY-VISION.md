**Web4City is a 3D virtual metaverse where developers ("vibe coders") explore, discover, and build together.**

| Gaming World | Web4City World | Purpose |

| 🎮 Video Game Console | 🤖 Your AI Assistant | Your tool to interact with the world |

## 🗺️ The 5 Districts

Web4City is divided into **5 themed districts**, each representing a different focus area of modern development:

### 1. 🧠 AI District
- **Color:** Pink (#e040c0)
- **Focus:** Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Neural Networks
- **Buildings:** AI tools, ML frameworks, automation repos
- **Vibe:** Futuristic, neural networks visualized, pink neon

### 2. ⛓️ Web3 District
- **Color:** Purple
- **Focus:** Blockchain, DeFi, NFTs, DAOs, Smart Contracts
- **Buildings:** dApps, protocols, wallet integrations
- **Vibe:** Decentralized, crypto-themed, purple glow

### 3. ⚛️ Quantum District
- **Color:** Cyan
- **Focus:** Post-Quantum Cryptography, Quantum Computing
- **Buildings:** Quantum algorithms, cryptography, future-tech
- **Vibe:** Sci-fi, quantum visualizations, cyan energy

### 4. 💰 VC Investment District
- **Color:** Orange
- **Focus:** Funding, Startups, Investment Opportunities
- **Buildings:** Startup projects, pitch decks, funding platforms
- **Vibe:** Business-meets-tech, orange warmth, opportunity

### 5. 📈 Growth District
- **Color:** Green
- **Focus:** Marketing, Community Building, Scaling, Growth Hacking
- **Buildings:** Marketing tools, analytics, community platforms
- **Vibe:** Energetic, growth charts, green vitality
